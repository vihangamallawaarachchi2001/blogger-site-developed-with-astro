Static Site Generation (SSG) isn't just a trend—it's the architectural foundation for resilient, fast, and secure websites. In this post, I break down how Astro leverages SSG to deliver near-instant loads, zero server costs, and immunity to common attack vectors like SSRF or injection flaws.

We’ll also compare build strategies, hydration models, and when to reach for SSR instead.
