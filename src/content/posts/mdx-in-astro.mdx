Gone are the days of plain Markdown. With MDX, you can embed live React components, interactive diagrams, and dynamic demos directly inside your blog posts. In this guide, I walk through setting up MDX in Astro, creating custom components like `<CodePen />` or `<Spoiler>`, and optimizing for both devX and performance.
