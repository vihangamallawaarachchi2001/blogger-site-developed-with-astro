Flash of Unstyled Content (FOUC) ruins the dark mode experience. The fix? A tiny inline script in `<head>` that reads `localStorage` or `prefers-color-scheme` before paint. This post shows the exact snippet, how to integrate with Tailwind, and how to sync user preference across devices.
